# PromptVerse Realism-First Content Overhaul

## Objective
Reframe PromptVerse as a premium marketplace of practical, commercial, realism-first prompts curated for real-world business and creative outcomes.

## Philosophy shifts
1. Diversity over repetition — no industry dominance; cybersecurity remains a minor specialty.
2. Realism over stylization — realistic photography and commercial image creation are defaults.
3. Practicality over novelty — LinkedIn headshots, product ads, wedding photography, real estate marketing, restaurant promotion, travel campaigns, movie posters, podcast branding, YouTube thumbnails, startup promotion, social media content, and similar use cases are prioritized.
4. Reference-aware prompting — prompts support uploaded-image workflows using explicit placeholders.
5. Expert-authored structure — prompts should read like they were written by photographers, marketers, filmmakers, designers, and AI specialists.

## Enforcement rules
- Any single industry target should remain under 5% of total prompts.
- Anime should remain around 2% of image prompts and never act as a default style.
- Unless explicitly requested, avoid anime, manga, cartoon, or illustration defaults.
- Image prompts should mention camera/lens/lighting/composition/environment/mood/style when appropriate.
- Reference-image prompt families must include face, character, product, style, pose, composition, outfit, brand, and environment workflows.
- No two prompts should feel templated for the same exact problem; examples must be meaningfully distinct.
